Programming at Sault College

The computer programming course at Sault College is a two year course - 4 semesters that will yield an Ontario College Diploma. During this course, you learn the hardware & software environments that mirror the industry standards that you will encounter in your career. After graduation, you have the option to go into

  1. Programming & Analysis
  2. Mobile App Design
  3. Network Security

These are the courses for the first semester of the course are:

  • CSD110
  • CSD111
  • CSD112
  • CMM115
  • MTH123
  • TNY130
